WHO WE ARE?

Solutionise is a multidisciplinary collaboration between business, law, IT students, and academics from LSBU. This business advice centre is designed to assist growth in small to medium-sized businesses in the local community. The Solutionise team strives to provide advice to everyone from kitchen table start-ups to established businesses to help them prosper. The scope of the team covers business plans, website building, and legal advice. Solutionise is an invaluable opportunity for students to learn in a practical setting, applying classroom skills whilst gaining an insight into their future careers to benefit our community.
